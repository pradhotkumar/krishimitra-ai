# Product Overview

KrishiMitra AI is a voice-first agricultural helpline for Indian farmers. It provides access to farming information through simple phone calls, eliminating the need for smartphones, internet, or digital literacy.

## Core Value Proposition

Farmers call a phone number and speak in Hindi to get:
- Government scheme information and eligibility
- Weather forecasts and alerts
- Crop cultivation guidance
- Fertilizer and pest management advice
- Market price information

## Target Users

Rural Indian farmers with:
- Basic mobile phones (no smartphone required)
- Limited or no internet connectivity
- Hindi as primary language
- Varying levels of digital literacy

## Key Differentiators

- Voice-only interface (no apps, no typing)
- Hindi language support with rural accent handling
- Works on basic phones over standard phone networks
- Accessible to farmers with low digital literacy
- Serverless architecture for scalability and cost-efficiency
